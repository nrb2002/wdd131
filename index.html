About Me

My name is Baron Tshibasu. I was born and grew up in Kinshasa in the Democratic Republic of Congo. I’m a member of the Church since 2003 and had the blessing of serving a full-time mission in West Africa from 2009 to 2011. I’m married and a father of three wonderful daughters who are 22, 9, and almost 1 :).

What you should know about the DRC

Nestled in the heart of Africa, the DRC is often described as the continent's revolver-shaped trigger. The country stands out for its size (3rd largest on the continent), natural wealth and bio-diversity.)
